--- Full description ---
Vidyarthi Lalkitab helps you create and save birth charts using Lal Kitab traditions.

Features:
• New kundli — name, date, time, city search
• Saved kundli — open, edit, delete
• Panchang — tithi, nakshatra, yoga, karana, sunrise/sunset
• Janma kundli — lagna and planets
• Varshfal annual chart
• 35-year Lal Kitab cycle
• Raja–Vazir–Dhoka (main planets)
• Hindi and English

Birth details stay on your device. Calculations work offline. Ads only on data-entry and saved-list screens — not on chart screens.

For students and practitioners of Lal Kitab astrology.
