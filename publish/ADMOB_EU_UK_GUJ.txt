=== UK / Europe (EU EEA) — Ad consent setup ===
App code (UMP SDK) is already in the project. You must finish AdMob Console setup.

--- STEP 1: AdMob Privacy & messaging (required) ---

1. Open https://admob.google.com
2. Select app: Vidyarthi Lalkitab
3. Go to: Privacy & messaging  (or GDPR / European regulations)
4. Create message:
   - Type: GDPR / User consent (UMP)
   - Regions: European Economic Area (EEA) + United Kingdom
   - Language: English (add Hindi if offered)
5. Options to include:
   - Personalized ads
   - Non-personalized ads (recommended to allow)
6. Publish the message

Without this step, EU/UK users may not see a consent form and ads may not fill.

--- STEP 2: Link AdMob app to Firebase (if not done) ---

AdMob → App settings → Link to Firebase project (Vidyarthi Lalkitab)
